Movie Webpage Project

I learned how to make a movie webpage by using all the knowledge of HTML and CSS into creating an movie poster call the "The Remnant Operation".

It's a horror movie that revolved around human experimentation. People from all over the world are invited to a scientific investigation! What will happened to them? People getting knock and taking to this mysterious Island which has desolate tree, dangerous obstacle course, and MANY MORE. The island is surrounded by a lethal forcefield that strinks!!! Will They Survive?

Over the course of my work, I think the most difficult aspects were plot brainstorming and design.
